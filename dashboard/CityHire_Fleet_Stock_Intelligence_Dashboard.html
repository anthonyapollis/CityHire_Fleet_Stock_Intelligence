Data grounding: category structure and 220+ product/model names (e.g. "Nifty Lift HR21 20.8m Hybrid Boom Lift", "Hilti TE 3000-AVR Heavy Breaker") were extracted from the public product catalogue at cityhire.co.uk on 2026-07-15 across 7 categories scraped in depth, extrapolated in the same style for the remaining 17 listed categories.
Important: all fleet quantities, stock codes, costs, dates, utilisation, repairs, approvals and transfers are SYNTHETIC — generated for this demonstration, not City Hire's real operational or financial data.
Companion deliverables: SQL analysis layer (17 queries), a formula-driven Excel workbook (Stock Master, Capex vs Budget, ROI Report, Approvals, Repairs, Stock Shortage, Min Stock Alerts), and this dashboard. Built by Anthony Apollis.
